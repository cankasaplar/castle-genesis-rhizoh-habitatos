# Current Questions / Guidance Areas

**Purpose:** Make it easy for advisors to see **where their judgment helps** — without implying we want unlimited scope on day one.

---

## How to use this document

We are not requesting open-ended consulting. We are identifying **specific lenses** where informed perspective would reduce risk before wider exposure. You may respond to any subset; partial guidance is welcome.

---

## We are currently looking for guidance regarding:

### Publication & communication readiness

- Whether our **research / controlled experiment** framing is appropriate before any public or semi-public narrative.  
- What should **not** be claimed externally while Phase 0.5–1 boundaries apply (e.g. “live world,” “global mesh,” implied user counts).  
- How to describe AI involvement without implying models **are** the system of record.

### Legal exposure (EU-facing awareness)

- High-level view of **KVKK / GDPR-aligned** posture for: static ingress, legal preamble, minimal future heartbeat class.  
- Whether “deployment without activation” is a useful distinction for counsel, or needs sharper public wording.  
- Timing: when to engage formal counsel relative to **READY** sign-off vs first signal probe.

### AI governance considerations

- Expectations for **human oversight**, documentation, and change control when LLMs assist UX or ops — not autonomous agent governance.  
- How advisors view **non-binding AI output** vs **sealed core state** separation.  
- Red flags we may be under-weighting in model/vendor churn scenarios.

### EU-facing regulatory awareness

- Whether our phased, opt-in, data-minimized signal design direction is **directionally sane** for EU users (not a compliance certificate).  
- Categories of processing we should **explicitly exclude** in Phase 1 to stay low-risk.  
- Any recommended **reference frameworks** (not legal advice) for documenting decisions.

### Infrastructure trust expectations

- What evidence partners or institutions typically expect **before** linking a custom domain or identity surface.  
- Whether our **two-gate** model (ready vs open) is credible to infrastructure reviewers.  
- Sensible **staging vs production** communication norms.

### Cybersecurity considerations

- Whether our **violation taxonomy** (state unchanged, no observation→control, tick integrity) is a useful ops language for security reviewers.  
- First-contact **responsible disclosure** posture if you recommend one.  
- Obvious gaps for a static+ingress deployment **without** live ingest (our current default).

### Organizational structure for future scaling

- Not fundraising structure — **operational** structure: who signs READY/HOLD, how legal/ops/engineering boundaries stay separated.  
- Whether a small-team **research initiative** should formalize an advisory board or counsel retainer before scale.  
- How to avoid **implicit** roles (e.g. moderation, DPO-like duties) falling on founders without planning.
